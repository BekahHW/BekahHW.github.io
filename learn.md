# Developer Growth E-Books

Welcome to a collection of e-books that distill years of experience, lessons learned, and modern perspectives on building a sustainable career in tech.

These aren't typical technical tutorials. Think of them as a cross between a course and a mentor's guidance—practical, actionable, and honest about the realities of developer growth in the AI era.

---

## Available E-Books

### [AI-Augmented Development: Your Competitive Advantage](/learn/ai-augmented-development/)

**A practical guide to using AI as a growth tool, not a replacement**

Learn how to choose AI tools that amplify your skills instead of replacing them, build workflows that compound value, and create a learning-first AI strategy that accelerates your growth without creating dependency.

**What you'll learn:**
- The three types of AI assistants and how to choose based on your learning style
- Context-aware guidance that actually helps you grow
- Building composed AI workflows that create compounding value
- Strategies for learning with AI without becoming dependent on it
- Modern perspectives on AI and junior developer growth

**Read it if:** You want to use AI to become a stronger developer, not just a faster coder.

[Start reading →](/learn/ai-augmented-development/)

---

### [From Bootcamp to Career: The Modern Developer's Journey](/learn/bootcamp-to-career/)

**A realistic guide to navigating your first developer role in the AI era**

Understand why the tech industry struggles with junior developers, how to stand out in a competitive market, what to look for in your first role, and how to grow from junior to mid-level developer—all while navigating the changing landscape of AI in development.

**What you'll learn:**
- The truth about the junior developer problem (and what you can do about it)
- How to stand out as a junior developer
- Green flags and red flags when evaluating companies
- Surviving the brutal reality of the job search
- Your first 90 days and growing to mid-level
- How AI changes the junior developer journey

**Read it if:** You're navigating the early stages of your developer career and want honest guidance.

[Start reading →](/learn/bootcamp-to-career/)

---

### [Building Community & Sustainable Development](/learn/community-and-sustainable-development/)

**A guide to thriving in tech without burning out—through community, boundaries, and sustainable practices**

Learn how to build and maintain healthy developer communities, set boundaries that protect your wellbeing, integrate mental health into your career approach, and create work practices that support long-term growth instead of burning you out.

**What you'll learn:**
- How to build authentic, supportive developer communities
- The identity crisis of professional community (and what it means for you)
- Mental health strategies for sustainable development
- Setting boundaries that protect your energy and wellbeing
- Integrating your whole life with your work (not sacrificing one for the other)
- Sharing knowledge and building in public

**Read it if:** You want to build a career that nourishes you instead of consuming you.

[Start reading →](/learn/community-and-sustainable-development/)

## Why These E-Books?

Over 7+ years of blogging, I've written about the realities of being a developer—the struggles, the growth, the burnout, the community, the AI revolution, and the journey from bootcamp to career.

These e-books synthesize:
- **144 blog posts** spanning career growth, AI workflows, community building, and mental health
- **Real experiences** from transitioning careers, starting a community during a pandemic, and navigating the AI era
- **Modern perspectives** on how AI, remote work, and changing expectations are reshaping developer growth
- **Practical strategies** that actually work, not just theory

Each e-book is designed to provide:
- **Actionable insights** you can apply immediately
- **Honest perspectives** about the challenges (not just the highlights)
- **Modern context** for navigating tech in 2025 and beyond
- **Course-like structure** with practical action plans
- **Value beyond tutorials** — these are about growing as a developer and a person
